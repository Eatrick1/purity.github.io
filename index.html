Our Story

A Ministry Built on
Truth and Revelation

Purified By Truth Ministries is led by Founder and Pastor Sheila Nalweyiso, with a clear conviction that the Church of Jesus Christ is victorious, that truth still transforms lives, and that every believer is called to walk in Kingdom authority, holiness and purpose.

From our base in Luwero, Uganda, the ministry holds regular Sunday and midweek services while also travelling to host crusades in surrounding communities — reaching new visitors with the Gospel and welcoming them into a growing local church family.

Founder & Pastor

Sheila Nalweyiso

Founder, Purified By Truth Ministries

Pastor Sheila Nalweyiso founded Purified By Truth Ministries out of a conviction to lead people into a genuine revelation of Jesus Christ as the Son of the Living God, and to disciple believers into spiritual maturity through the uncompromising teaching of Scripture.

Under her leadership the ministry holds regular services in Luwero and travels to surrounding communities to host crusades, declaring the Gospel and inviting new believers into the local church family.

Our Purpose

Vision & Mission

Vision

Our Vision

To raise a generation purified by the truth of God's Word, established upon the revelation of Jesus Christ, equipped with Kingdom authority, and commissioned to transform nations for the glory of God.

Mission

Our Mission

To proclaim God's truth, reveal Jesus Christ as Lord and Savior, disciple believers into spiritual maturity, equip them to exercise Kingdom authority, and build a victorious Church that advances God's Kingdom on earth.
